# BoxTimer — User Guide

## Install (Android Chrome)

1. Open the BoxTimer URL in Chrome.
2. Tap the ⋮ menu → **Add to Home screen** / **Install app**.
3. Launch it from your home screen — it runs full-screen in portrait.

## Create a workout by voice

1. Tap **New Workout**.
2. Tap the microphone and describe your workout, e.g.
   *"30 seconds prep, 5 rounds, first 2 at 30 seconds, 4th round 40 seconds,
   last round 20 seconds, rest 15 seconds."*
3. Tap the microphone again to stop. BoxTimer parses it and reads the config
   back to you.
4. Tap **Start**, then **Save** (give it a name) or **Skip** to start without
   saving. Tap **Re-record** to try again.

## Run a saved workout

- On the home screen, tap a saved card to load and start it.
- Long-press a card to delete it.

## During the timer

- **Tap anywhere** to pause/resume.
- Tap **✕** (top-right) to stop and return home (asks to confirm).
- The 🔆 icon means the screen will stay awake.
- Audio cues: a bell starts each round, two beeps start a rest, three short
  beeps count down the last 3 seconds, and a triple bell signals completion.

## When the workout finishes

- **Repeat** restarts the same workout.
- **Home** returns to the home screen.
